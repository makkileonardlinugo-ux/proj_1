Why it feels different

Devices that hand off, not hand over.

Tester Tech runs one operating system across the whole lineup. Your data is processed on the device that captured it, and shared between your gear, never the cloud, unless you say so.

See it on the Smart Watch Pro →
  • Continuous handoff. Start a call on your watch, finish it on your glasses, no pairing dance.
  • On-device intelligence. Every model runs locally, so latency is low and your data stays yours.
  • One health graph. Ring, watch and buds write to a single secure record you fully control.
  • Open standards. Thread and Matter built in, so it plays well with the gear you already own.
